About Us.

Our Vision

Our Vision is to be the leading education consultancy firm that bring skills, ideas and expertise into school management for all round development of the Nigerian child making school business easy and enjoyable for Proprietors.

Our Aims and Objectives

  • To inspire school Owners/Administrators through Seminars, Workshops and Conferences towards challenging them to run a 21st century school irrespective of location.
  • To unite private schools and create the spirit of positive competition by organizing inter school competitions such as Debate, Quiz, Brain Teasers etc.
  • To recruit teachers and to deploy same to the schools that need them.
  • To organize regular Teachers Training programs aimed at equipping teachers with 21st century teaching models for excellence.
  • TTo manage schools where the proprietor is not around or requires our expertise
  • To organize sponsored educative scholarship competitions such as Quiz Competition, Spelling Champ, Maths Champ, Reading Champ etc.
  • To organize educational festivals for school children aimed at awakening and maximizing the potential of the young ones.

What We do?

Inspiration and guidance

By organizing seminars, workshops, and conferences, STSOC aims to inspire and challenge school owners and administrators to run 21st-century schools. This can include sharing best practices, innovative ideas, and strategies for success.

Community building

STSOC aims to unite private schools and create a spirit of positive competition through inter-school competitions such as debates, quizzes, and brain teasers. By bringing schools together in this way, STSOC can help build a sense of community and promote excellence.

Teacher training

STSOC organizes regular training programs to help teachers Develop the skills and knowledge needed to teach in the 21st century. This can include training in new teaching models, technology integration, and other relevant topics.

School management

STSOC can provide management services to schools that need additional support or expertise. This can include helping schools improve their operations, streamline processes, and achieve their goals.

Scholarship competitions

STSOC organizes scholarship competitions such as quizzes, spelling bees, math competitions, and reading competitions. These events can help promote academic excellence and provide opportunities for students to win scholarships and other prizes.

Educational festivals

STSOC organizes festivals for school children aimed at awakening and maximizing their potential. These festivals can provide opportunities for students to showcase their talents, learn new skills, and engage in a range of educational activities.
